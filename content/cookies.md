# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Puntuale uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot refuse these cookies without affecting website functionality.

**Examples:**
- Session cookies for secure login
- Load balancing cookies
- Security tokens

### Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

**Examples:**
- Remembering your preferences
- Language settings
- Regional settings

### Analytical Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

**Examples:**
- Google Analytics
- Page view tracking
- User behavior analysis

### Marketing Cookies
These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements based on your interests.

**Examples:**
- LinkedIn ads
- Google Ads
- Retargeting pixels

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

- **Security**: To protect against fraudulent activity and ensure user safety
- **Performance**: To understand and improve website performance
- **Analytics**: To analyze usage patterns and user behavior
- **Personalization**: To remember your preferences and settings
- **Advertising**: To deliver relevant advertisements

## Managing Your Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies.

### Browser Settings
Most web browsers allow you to:
- View what cookies are stored
- Delete existing cookies
- Block third-party cookies
- Block cookies from specific websites
- Delete cookies when you close your browser

### Disabling Cookies
Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not function correctly, and you may lose access to personalized content.

### Third-Party Cookies
You can opt out of third-party cookies by visiting the opt-out pages of major advertising networks:
- [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)
- [LinkedIn Ad Preferences](https://www.linkedin.com/psettings/guest-controls/retargeting-opt-out)

## Cookie Duration

Cookies can be classified by their duration:

- **Session cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies**: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
